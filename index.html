Tools I used to create the game

GIMP is a cross-platform image editor available for GNU/Linux, OS X, Windows and more operating systems. It is free software, you can change its source code and distribute your changes.

Target Demographics

The target audience for the game would be kids. The age range can be 3 years old and above.

Personality should be playful, lovely and somebody who loves Christmas and Winter decorations. The game attracts new players because I create all the designs, and this game attracts people who love the cold winter atmosphere, but also enjoy the warm atmosphere inside.

Not only that, it also brings the Halloween 's fear and adventure spirit, because the game begins at the beginning of Halloween. Players want to play this game if they love adventure and want to be a hero for all children to save Christmas and wake up Santa Claus.

Levels

There are three main levels in this game. Each level has one main evil that the main character needed to defeat. Each evil has its especial damage ability.

  1. Rock
  2. Fire
  3. Snow

First level, this is where the main character gets out of his home and starts to find the gifts in a cold dark night. Here Peter must climb up the mountain on the dirty rock and dirty dust road. At this level, Peter must face with THE ROCK EVIL who uses rock to damage him.

Second level is where Peter goes through a forest on his way to the mountain. Here he must go through a tunnel and faces THE FIRE EVIL which uses fire to damage and kill Peter.

The third level is where Peter meets a winter storm and there is snow everywhere. Here Peter is finally got to the mountain and faces his last fight with THE SNOW EVIL. Once he defeats the evil, he can collect all the gifts and saves Christmas of all Children in the world. Cheer for Peter!
